The problem

Three things make Genie setup fragile before launch.

⚠

No quality loop

Answers go live without grounded evaluation. No baseline, no score, no way to know if what shipped is trustworthy.

✍

Manual authoring

Business framing, instructions, joins, and context assembled by hand every time. Fragile. Inconsistent. Unrepeatable.

⚙

Raw warehouse complexity

No semantic buffer between warehouse tables and the Genie experience. One schema change breaks the space.

Most teams are hand-assembling business framing, instructions, joins, and quality checks. That means the experience may launch, but the trust model is still weak. So the answer is not another black box. The answer is a controlled build path.
