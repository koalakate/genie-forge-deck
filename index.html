The problem

Three things make Genie setup fragile before launch.

False Simplicity

Launching Genie is easy, but setting it up well is not obvious, it requires proper expertise.

Routine Validation

Roughly 75% of the effort happens after setup, when teams test real questions, compare ground truth, and tune answers.

Weeks, Not Clicks

One trusted space can take 4-6 weeks of SME-led setup, evaluation, and iterative tuning before production.

Most teams are hand-assembling business framing, instructions, joins, and quality checks. That means the experience may launch, but the trust model is still weak. So the answer is not another black box. The answer is a controlled build path.
